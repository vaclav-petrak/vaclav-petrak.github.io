Václav Petrák

I am a data specialist with a background in biomedical engineering. I focus on modeling, statistics, and complex data analysis. I have spent over a decade in academic and industrial R&D, working on ophthalmic and material science projects. I have gained recognition for my analytical and visualization skills.

For the past decade, I have been active in teaching students and fostering interdisciplinary collaboration within the scientific community. I often served as a bridge between researchers from different fields.

During my career, I have seen excellent ideas go unrecognized due to unclear communication, poor visualization, or cluttered slides. Experts tend to have a deep knowledge of their work but often suffer from the "curse of knowledge"—they forget what it's like for outsiders not sharing their insights. I solve this by creating designs that respect the complexity of your data while guiding your audience to the correct conclusion without ambiguity. As a generalist, I offer more than just design. I provide the analytical backbone your project needs—from cleaning complex datasets and building statistical models to gathering verified intelligence.
